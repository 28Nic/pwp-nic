Nic's Personal Website Project Milestone 1

Purpose

My personal website project will help me connect with fellow fiber artists and showcase my skills as a professional web developer.

Audience

Primary Audience: My main focus is on building connection with other fiber artists. Secondary Audience: This project should secondarily serve as an example of my web development skills to share with potential employers and fellow web developers.

Goal

This project will act as a gallery of my art to share with my network and beyond.

Persona: Corin Taylor - Hobby Knitter and Professional Web Developer

Technology

Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra with Android 13. Dell XPS 13 2022 with Windows 11. Favors Firefox as his favorite browser. Proficient technology user.

Love/Hate

  • Loves discovering new artists and browsing their work for inspiration.
  • Loves interacting with content from other web developers.
  • Loves when his personal passions intersect with his professional ones.
  • Hates having to rely on social media platforms to browse art and connect with other artists.
  • Hates the formality of traditional networking sites.

Attitudes and Needs

  • Corin wants to engage with fellow fiber artists but finds the metrics-driven social media platforms to be alienating.
  • Corin wants more engaging knitting content in his life and finds social media's bite-sized snippets limiting of the art.
  • Corin wants to expand his network as a web developer outside traditional online and in-person spaces.
  • Still early in his web career, Corin wants to grow his web development skill set and find inspiration for personal and professional projects.

User Story

As a hobby knitter and junior web developer, I'm looking for content that marries my passions with my profession.
